Green Line 1.


Particulars

Chaohu Haitian Shippingbuilding Co Ltd pleased to have been selected to construct the Catamaran Passenger Ferry MV Green Line 1 for a private client.

The vessel operates passenger-only service from Teknaf to Saint Martin island. She is suitable for sightseeing and transportation use or short trips in costal area, for 120 passengers including 14 persons on upper deck.
